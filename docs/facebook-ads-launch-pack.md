# Facebook Ads Launch Pack

## Objective
Launch a first Meta static-ad pack built around Animalia's highest-intent categories:
- omega / fish oil
- calming
- joint support
- shop by need

These creatives are designed for direct-response traffic and purchase testing, not brand-only reach.

## Asset Folder
Saved ad files live in:

`public/ads/facebook/`

## Recommended Campaign Structure

### Campaign 1
Name: `Animalia | Purchase | Broad Testing | Statics`

Ad sets:
- `Broad | Dogs + Cats | 25-54`
- `Interest | Pet wellness + supplements`
- `Interest | Anxiety, joint health, fish oil`
- `Retargeting | 30d site visitors`

Optimization:
- Sales / Purchase
- 7-day click attribution to start
- Advantage+ placements on

- `fb-calming-square-01.png`

- `fb-joint-square-01.png`
- `fb-omega-portrait-01.png`

Why:

- better once pixel has some learning

## Offer Guidance
For early spend, keep the on-image offer light and let the landing page do the heavier selling.

If you test a promotional version later, use one of:
- `Free shipping over $50`
- `Shop top wellness needs`
- `Curated from trusted brands`

Avoid stacking too many claims in the image.
