Animalia: Headless Brand & Business Blueprint
Frontend-Owned Brand • Shopify-Powered Commerce
1. Executive Summary
Animalia is a premium, intentionally curated pet wellness brand built as a custom, headless e-commerce experience.
The brand lives on a bespoke, high-performance Next.js storefront, while Shopify functions as the backend for:
Product database
SKU & variant management
Pricing & inventory
Checkout, taxes, and payments
Order creation & fulfillment triggers
Animalia positions itself as the “Whole Foods of Pet Care” — a high-trust, clean, and emotionally resonant marketplace for modern pet parents who treat pets as family.
The storefront is fully custom-coded for feel, aesthetics, and performance.
The catalog, however, is sourced, maintained, and fulfilled entirely through Shopify + suppliers.

5. Business Model (Headless Architecture)
Element	Details
Frontend	Custom Next.js storefront (your “vibe-coded” experience)
Backend	Shopify as the commerce engine
Sourcing	Output of Shopify import apps + vetted wholesalers
Checkout	Shopify Checkout (secure, taxes handled)
Fulfillment	Dropshipper integrations triggered by Shopify orders
Bundles	Created in Shopify, displayed beautifully on frontend
Margins	50–80% markup; subscription products improve LTV
Retention	Email/SMS flows + personalized bundles
Support	Human, warm, honest tone
